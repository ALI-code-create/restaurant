Our Story

A tradition of excellence since 1998

Where It All Began

Founded in 1998 by chef and restaurateur Henri Moreau, La Maison d'Or was born from a dream to bring the grandeur of European fine dining to the heart of North Africa. What started as a small bistro with twelve tables soon became one of the most celebrated restaurants in the region.

Henri's philosophy has always been simple: honor the ingredient, respect the tradition, and never stop innovating. Three decades later, our kitchen still follows these principles with every plate that leaves the pass.

Our Philosophy

We believe that dining is not merely eating — it is an experience that engages all the senses. From the warm glow of our chandeliers to the aroma of freshly baked bread, every detail is designed to transport our guests to a world of elegance and comfort.

Our suppliers are chosen with the same care as our ingredients: local farmers, sustainable fisheries, and artisan producers who share our commitment to quality and ethical practices.

Meet Our Chef

Chef Antoine Bisset

With over twenty years of experience in Michelin-starred kitchens across Paris, London, and Tokyo, Chef Antoine Bisset brings a world of technique and creativity to La Maison d'Or. His cooking is a dialogue between classic French tradition and bold global influences.

"For me, cooking is about memory — the taste of your grandmother's stew, the crunch of a perfect baguette, the sigh of satisfaction after an exceptional meal. I strive to create those moments for every guest."
