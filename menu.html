Our Menu

Crafted with passion, served with elegance

Our menu is a carefully curated selection of dishes that honor Italian tradition while embracing contemporary technique. Each item is crafted from the finest seasonal ingredients — from the vine-ripened tomatoes in our Bruschetta to the Nebbiolo grapes in our Barolo DOCG. We work closely with local farms, sustainable fisheries, and artisan producers to ensure every plate meets the highest standards of quality and flavor.
